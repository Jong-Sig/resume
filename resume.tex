\section{Research Interest}
\textbf{Keywords}{: Algorithms; Human Interaction; Collaboration and Competition; Knowledge and Innovation; Platform Strategy}

\vspace{4pt}
We are living through a shift in which algorithm-driven technologies are transforming how humans interact, collaborate, compete, and innovate. This raises two questions that guide my research: (1) \textit{how do algorithms shape human interaction, collaboration, and competition?} and (2) \textit{what does it mean for knowledge and innovation in organizations?}
